Residential Roofing

Walker Metals can provide you with the panels cut to your specification saving you time and energy. Whether you are repairing your storage shed or building a new one, residential panels may be what you need. Long lasting metal panels come in Galvalume "Plus" or a variety of colors. Metal panels protect residents from the weather elements: hail and wind. Metal does not burn, protecting you from wind driven sparks.

Because we use only high quality metal coils, colored metal roofing and siding is warranted against cracking, chipping or rusting for a period of 40 years. Galvalume "Plus" comes with an acrylic finish, which provides the panels with a longer, newer look and comes with a 20 year warranty against rusting.

We have a trim shop for your special trim needs. If you can draw it, we can usually make it while you wait. We make all the accessories needed for your project.

Commercial Roofing

During the course of a large commercial construction project, things can change quickly. The project’s budget and design may go through a number of alterations even after the construction is underway. You need to be able to respond quickly to these changes.

Whether you’re installing a new storefront or building a boat storage facility, getting all the metal materials you need from one location is crucial. A large project might require a variety of products and styles, but you can’t afford to make extra trips to several manufacturers. It cost you valuable time and money.

You CAN get the materials you need and stay on budget at the same time if you call Walker Metals.

Just place your new order by 2 pm - we can typically have your panels manufactured and cut to length by the next business morning. And just because you receive your order fast doesn’t mean it’s rushed. We cut our 26 and 29 gauge metal to your specific requirements. Our helpful staff works closely with you to make sure every panel meets the exact dimensions you specify. You might call it the Walker Metals advantage. We just call it the way we do business.

Visit our 23,000 square foot operating facility in the Walker Industrial Park or call the friendly folks at Walker Metals to handle all your commercial roofing needs!

Metal Buildings

Our metal buildings are pre-engineered to the customer's specifications. Wind loads are calculated using the requirements of the local government specifications. The closer to the Gulf, the greater the wind load requirements.

Panel Profiles

We manufacture two different panel profiles.


PBR Panel:

(PB) "R" Panel is a common metal building industry profile used both for roof and wall applications. It has a PB (Purlin Bearing) leg. Available in 26 Gauge Only.


Residential Panel(low profile)

Residential Panels comes in low profile wall and roofing panels. This profile is used primarily for residential roofing, storage facilities, barns and light commercial/industrial buildings.Available in 26 and 29 Gauge.
