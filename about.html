About Us

The Estave family has been selling metal roofing and metal siding in Livingston Parish and surrounding areas since 2003.

The Estave family purchased land in the new Walker Industrial Park located in Walker, La. and called their facility WALKER METALS and built over a 20,000 square foot facility using the state of the art computer operated form roller. Since then we have been custom cutting metal panels to meet your roofing and siding needs. Whether you are in need of an energy saving metal roof or metal siding and roofing for your personal workshop, or a pre-engineered metal building, we are here to provide you with your metal needs.

No matter the job, we custom cut our panels to meet your specific requirements. We can roll form your metal panels as short as 3 feet and as long as you can haul them. We cut your panels to the nearest 1/2 inch. If you need your panels manufactured 8'6" that is what you get and that is all you pay for.

The manufacturing facility is in close proximity to I-12 allowing us to better serve our customers in the surrounding areas. The new facility has easy access off Florida Blvd (Hwy 190), Walker North (Hwy 447) just a few miles from I-12 and a couple of hundred yards from Corbin Blvd (Hwy 449) at Florida Blvd.

We make all of your roofing trim right here at Walker Metals and use only 26 gauge material to make your metal roofing trim and flashings.

The loading of your truck or trailer is under roof and out of the weather. Drive straight in on one side, load under roof, and drive out the other side of the building.

If you place your order by 2PM one business day, it will be available for pick up the next business morning. Sometimes if you call early in the morning we can have your order ready later that same day.

We are the friendly, helpful folks.
