About Us

Since 2003, Walker Metals is the one-stop shop for all your metal roofing and siding needs.

Our metal products meet a variety of needs whether you are a homeowner, installer or contractor. We offer 26 and 29 gauge metal in two panel profiles. So no matter what your new building or roofing project requires, we have got you covered with high-grade Galvalume Plus or a wide selection of colors.

Cut to length custom orders, next day availability if ordered by 2pm and a variety of accessories to finish the job. Because at Walker Metals, customer service is our number one priority!
